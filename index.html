The development

Address: 22 Sui Wo Road, Fo Tan, Shatin, N.T., Hong Kong

Basic Information of HKBU Staff Quarter

Built Upon the hillside of Fo Tan at Shatin and attracts the scenery of Shatin Town Centre, the development was built by the University and occupied in Autumn 1994.

It composed a total of 113 units in 5 Towers with 8 storeys in each Tower.

  • All 5 Towers were used as the staff quarters until end of June, 2000.
  • two towers (Tower 4 & Tower 5) were returned to the government in July, 2000.
  • At present, Tower 1 to Tower 3 are used as the staff quarters while Tower 4 & Tower 5 are used as government quarters for civil servants.

The Apartment

There are a total of 68 apartments in Tower 1 to Tower 3 with 2 to 4 bedrooms and gross floor area ranging from 100 sq.m. to 170 sq.m.

Utilities

Electricity, water and gas meters are installed and ready for use.

TV

Local TV and satellite TV channels are available.

Telecommunication services

Telecommunication services ports have been provided in each flat.

Other services

Residents can apply for connections of telephone line, fax line, Internet services, IDD services and cable TV.

Furniture Provided

Standard furniture and an arrival kit

Blankets and bedsheets, kitchen utensils and daily consumables are provided for each flat.

Additional furniture items will be provided to visiting and exchange scholars living in the Quarters.

Car park

There are a total of 85 car parking spaces (60 covered spaces and 25 open spaces) for use by residents with valid Parking Permit issued by the Property Management Company.

All parking spaces owned by the University are marked by yellow border. Residents are advised to observe the carpark regulations and parked in parking spaces marked yellow.

Guests parking is also available upon registration at the entrance guard house.

Facilities

The Staff Quarters is featured with recreational facilities like swimming pool,
fitness room, squash court, table tennis court and children’s playground.
Two common rooms are also available for resident’s use on reservation.
There are car parking spaces for residents with valid Parking Permit issued by the property management company of the Staff Quarters.

Frequently Asked Questions

You can find out the solution of your inquiries below before contacting us.

  • Can I know the price of the apartment?

    Please contact Mr. W. C. Koo at 3411 5650 for more details.

  • Is there any public transport going to the staff quarter?

    You can take minibus route 69K from Shatin MTR station. The staff quarter is 15 minutes away from the MTR station. So, it is very convenient.

  • I am not a staff at HKBU, can I rent the house?

    Yes. Since two of the towers are owned by the government for civil servants, you can rent them although you are not a HKBU staff.
    For more detail, you can visit the Government Property Agency website. https://www.gpa.gov.hk/english/let/suiwo.html
